Key Takeaways: Profiling vs. Prediction

Prediction

"Can I segment a new customer?"
This objective focuses on assigning new observations to existing segments to predict behavior (e.g., Will this person churn? Is this transaction fraudulent?).

Profiling

"What defines my customers?"
This objective is about understanding the characteristics that create the segments in the first place. It tells you why groups are different (e.g., Loyal customers buy weekly).
