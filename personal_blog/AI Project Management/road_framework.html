A Leader’s Guide to the R.O.A.D. Framework

A Strategic Methodology for Scoping, Planning, and Executing AI at Scale

Developed by Ian McCulloh & taught at Johns Hopkins University

Core Philosophy

The Problem

AI projects fail due to lack of governance, vague goals, and treating engineering like a "science project" without structure.

The Solution

R.O.A.D. enforces Division of Labor, Accountability, and Risk Management to lower costs and manage expectations.

Why R.O.A.D. Works

Reduces "Hype"

Forces leaders to validate the problem actually requires AI before investing.

Enforces Accountability

Everyone knows their specific role. Domain Experts don't write code; Scientists don't define business goals.

Mitigates Risk

Explicit steps for bias assessment and concept drift monitoring ensure longevity.
