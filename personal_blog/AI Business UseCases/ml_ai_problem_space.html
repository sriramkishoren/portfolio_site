Why Structure Matters: Key Principles

Crucial concepts for designing the AI problem space.

Success Factor

Structuring determines the success of the algorithm, not just its speed.

What is Structuring?
Translating a vague real-world problem into a specific mathematical task (Input → Output → Goal).
Common Misconception
"Structuring eliminates the need for data"

Design Priority

The most influential factor is the clarity of objectives. Without clear success criteria, the problem space cannot be structured correctly.

Success Stories

Healthcare Operations

The Infusion Pump Shortage

Problem: Hospital staff couldn't find infusion pumps, leading to perceived shortages and expensive new purchases.

Solution: Mapped the "social journey" of pumps. Realized shortages were due to lack of social ties between departments. Created training classes mixing nurses from different wards.

Result: Automated "sharing economy" without orderlies.
Financial Equity

Loans for the Undocumented

Problem: ITIN applicants (undocumented immigrants) lack standard credit history/driver's licenses but need business loans.

Solution: A credit union built a custom credit scoring model using alternative data points specific to this population.

Result: Zero defaults in 2 years of the program.
Defense / Counter-Terrorism

Left-of-Boom Detection

Problem: Attacks happen after planning. Detecting the explosion is too late.

Solution: Used post-blast data (fingerprints, electronics) to map "illicit supply chains" and social networks of bomb makers.

Result: Disrupted networks during planning phase.
