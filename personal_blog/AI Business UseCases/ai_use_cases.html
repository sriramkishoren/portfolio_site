The Modern AI Landscape

A Guide to the 9 Pillars of Artificial Intelligence & Their Real-World Impact

Computer Vision

Enables machines to interpret and act on visual data using CNNs.

Top Use Cases

  • •Medical: Detecting tumors in X-rays with accuracy often exceeding humans.
  • •Retail: Checkout-free stores tracking items automatically.
  • •Safety: Construction site monitoring for hard hats.

Natural Language Processing

Bridges the gap between raw text/speech and actionable intelligence using transformers.

Top Use Cases

  • •Global: Real-time translation breaking language barriers.
  • •Finance: Analyzing earnings calls to predict stock movements.
  • •Healthcare: Extracting diagnoses from physician notes.

Graph Analytics

Models data as networks to reveal relationships traditional databases miss.

Top Use Cases

  • •Fraud: Detecting money laundering through circuitous routes.
  • •Pharma: Identifying drug interactions and new therapeutics.
  • •Supply Chain: Predicting disruption risks and dependencies.

Data Classification

Automatically assigning data points to categories using supervised learning.

Top Use Cases

  • •Email: Spam filters achieving accuracy above 99%.
  • •Banking: Assessing credit risk for loan approvals.
  • •HR: Screening resumes to categorize candidates by fit.

Forecasting

Predicting future events based on historical time-series data.

Top Use Cases

  • •Manufacturing: Predictive maintenance cuts costs 10-25%.
  • •Energy: Predicting wind/solar generation for grid balance.
  • •Retail: Preventing stockouts and overstock scenarios.

Interactive Chat

Engaging users in natural dialogue using Large Language Models (LLMs).

Top Use Cases

  • •Support: Resolving 30-40% of tickets without humans.
  • •Education: Always-available AI tutors for students.
  • •E-commerce: Virtual shopping assistants guiding purchases.

Autonomous Robots

Machines integrating perception, decision-making, and action to operate independently.

Top Use Cases

  • •Transport: Self-driving taxis operating in cities.
  • •Surgery: Minimally invasive procedures via da Vinci system.
  • •Logistics: 24/7 warehouse robots sorting packages.

Generative AI

Creating new content (code, images, text) rather than analyzing existing data.

Top Use Cases

  • •Coding: Tools like Copilot used by 50% of developers.
  • •Pharma: Creating candidate molecules in weeks.
  • •Media: Generating reports and copy instantly.

Symbolic AI

Explicit representation of knowledge through logic and rules (IF/THEN).

Top Use Cases

  • •Compliance: Verifying flight plans against regulations.
  • •Finance: Generating audit trails for regulatory approval.
  • •Automation: Strict rule-based payroll/accounting.

The Future is Hybrid

"The most powerful applications optimize performance by combining these technologies—such as Computer Vision feeding Symbolic Systems, or Generative AI creating training data for Classifiers."
